About Us

POH - organization offering a range of training programs to young Afghans, providing them with the resources to develop their skills.

The POH, is committed to becoming a hub for excellence in youth development. The center aspires to empower the younger generation by offering comprehensive training modules that cover a spectrum of disciplines, ranging from professional skills to personal development. POH envisions a future where Afghanistan’s youth emerge not just as skilled professionals but as visionary leaders capable of navigating the complexities of a dynamic global landscape.

Our Services

At Path of Hope (POH), we empower Afghan girls through quality education designed to address the real challenges they face. Our programs offer practical skills, strong mentorship, and a supportive environment for personal growth. By choosing us, you join a movement dedicated to building a brighter and more inclusive future.

POH Vision

Our vision is to create a future where Afghan girls have equal access to education and opportunities in various fields. We aim to build a society where girls are empowered with skills to succeed in the global world. By offering specialised boot camps and training, we support them in breaking barriers and becoming leaders. POH envisions a generation of confident girls driving positive change in Afghanistan and beyond.

POH Mission

Our mission is to empower Afghan youth, especially girls, through high-quality education in diverse disciplines. We focus on subjects like mathematics, computer science, languages, public speaking, and reading clubs. POH creates a supportive space where students learn from successful individuals through seminars and mentorship. We are committed to excellence, inclusivity, and developing strong, moral leaders for the future.

Our Team Members

Meet the people powering Path of Hope with passion and purpose.

Zohal Noori

Founder

Zohal Noori is the founder of Path of Hope and a student of international relations at Lewis University. She has received ten scholarships to study at top U.S. universities and collaborates with over 13 organizations, including the UN and Rotaract. Passionate about education, she launched the organization to empower Afghan girls. In her free time, she enjoys reading and exploring diverse perspectives.

Nematullah Bayat

Co-Founder

Nematullah Bayat is the co-founder of Path of Hope and a Master’s student in Water and Environmental Engineering in Germany. He speaks five languages and uses his global communication skills to support Afghan girls' access to education. His passion for learning and development aligns with the organization’s vision. He is committed to building a brighter, educated future for Afghan youth.

Nelofar Noori

Project Manager

Nelofar Noori is a pre-healthcare student in the U.S. and serves as Project Manager at POH. She helps shape the organization’s strategic direction and oversees training topics and planning. As a tutor at American River College, she supports immigrant students through academic and enrollment guidance. She is dedicated to humanitarian work, peace-building, and bridging educational gaps.

Zainab Noori

Head of Communication

Zainab Noori is an Afghan international relations student living in Kabul and serves as Head of Communication at POH. She manages contact with trainers and organizes training programs. Zainab is deeply engaged in humanitarian efforts and enjoys connecting through books and nature. Her role helps ensure effective communication and smooth coordination within the organization.

Aman Shah Kabiri

Advisor

Aman Shah Kabiri is an advisor at POH, studying Computer Science at AUAF and Public Policy at Kabul University. He is a women’s rights activist and education advocate who supports girls through digital learning. His unique blend of technology and policy drives innovative change in Afghan education. He’s passionate about using knowledge to create lasting social impact.
